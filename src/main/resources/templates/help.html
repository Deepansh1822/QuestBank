Getting Started

Learn the basics of QuestBank, how to set up your account, and manage your first question bank.

To get started:

  • Complete your profile in the Settings page.
  • Navigate to 'Add Question' to start building your repository.
  • Use 'Create Paper' to generate your first test.

Adding Questions

Guide on adding questions manually, using AI generation, or importing from PDF documents.

Methods supported:

  • Manual: Fill out the form with question text, options, and metadata.
  • AI Gen: Provide a topic name, and our AI will generate questions for you.
  • PDF Import: (Coming Soon) Upload existing question papers to extract questions.

Creating Papers

Step-by-step tutorial on generating professional question papers from your bank.

Go to the Create Paper page:

  1. Select the Subject, Class, and Difficulty mix.
  2. Defin total marks and sections.
  3. Click 'Generate' to download a professionally formatted PDF.

Account Management

How to update your profile, change password, and manage organization settings.

Visit your Profile Page to:

  • Upload a new profile picture.
  • Update your organization name (will appear on papers).
  • Change your password securely.

Security & Privacy

Information about how we protect your data and ensure secure access.

We use industry-standard encryption for passwords and data. Your question bank is private to your user account and cannot be accessed by others.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and how to resolve them quickly.

Common Fixes:

  • PDF Download Failed: Ensure you have enough questions in the bank matching your criteria.
  • Login Issues: Use the 'Forgot Password' link or contact support.
