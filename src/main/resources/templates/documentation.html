Introduction

Welcome to QuestBank, your intelligent partner for question bank management and automated paper generation. Designed for educators, schools, and coaching institutes, QuestBank streamlines the tedious process of creating exams.

With QuestBank, you can build a robust repository of questions across various subjects, valid difficulties, and formats. Our advanced AI integration even helps you generate new questions from raw text, ensuring your bank never runs dry.

Quick Start Guide

Get up and running in minutes:

  1. Sign Up/Login: Access your secure dashboard.
  2. Add Questions: Use the "Add Question" feature to populate your database manually or via AI.
  3. Create Paper: Navigate to "Create Paper", select your criteria (Subject, Class, Difficulty), and let the system generate a formatted PDF.
  4. Download: Export your paper and solution key instantly.
Tip: Start by adding at least 10 questions per subject to generate meaningful papers.

Managing Questions

Manual Entry

To add a question manually:

  1. Question Text: Enter the main question stem clearly.
  2. Options: Provide 4 distinct options (for MCQs). Mark the correct answer.
  3. Metadata: Select the Subject, Class (e.g., 10th, 12th), Topic, and Difficulty Level (Easy, Medium, Hard).

Correct metadata is crucial for the random paper generator to work effectively.

AI Generation

Leverage the power of AI (powered by Groq Llama models) to create questions automatically.

How to use:

  • Enter a Subject and Topic (e.g., "History", "World War II").
  • Select the number of questions and difficulty.
  • Click "Generate". The AI will produce a list of questions which you can review and save to your bank.
Beta Feature: The AI model is optimized for general academic subjects. Always review generated content for accuracy.

PDF Import (Coming Soon)

We are working on a feature to scan PDF textbooks or previous year papers and automatically extract questions into your repository. Stay tuned for updates!

Generating Papers

Settings & Templates

When creating a paper, you define the structure:

Parameter Description
Total Marks Set the maximum marks for the paper.
Sections Divide paper into sections (e.g., Section A: MCQs, Section B: Theory).
Difficulty Mix Adjust the ratio of Easy, Medium, and Hard questions (e.g., 30:50:20).

Export Formats

QuestBank currently supports exporting papers in:

  • PDF: Professional, print-ready format with school header and logo.
  • Word (DOCX): Editable format for last-minute manual tweaks (Planned).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes, all question data is stored securely. Your question bank is private to your organization.

Can I add images to questions?

Currently, we support text-based questions. Image support for diagrams and charts is on our roadmap.

How many papers can I generate?

There is no limit to the number of papers you can generate on the standard plan.
